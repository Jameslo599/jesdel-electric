ABOUT JESDEL ELECTRIC

At Jesdel Electric, we take pride in being Fort Worth’s trusted local electrician. With years of hands-on experience, we provide safe, reliable, and affordable electrical services for homes and businesses across the area.

Our mission is simple: to keep your lights on, your systems running smoothly, and your family safe. From minor repairs and troubleshooting to full wiring projects and panel upgrades, we deliver workmanship you can count on—done right the first time.

We believe in building lasting relationships with our customers through honesty, professionalism, and exceptional service. Whether it’s an emergency call or a planned project, you can rely on us to show up on time, respect your property, and complete the job with care.

At the heart of what we do is a commitment to serving our Fort Worth community with integrity and excellence.

My name is Alberto Delacruz I was raised in a humble migrant family. I grew up through a lot of struggles. When I was attending High School due to the hardship I had to work two part time jobs in the fast-food industry to have enough money to pay for my expenses.

In 2004 I graduated from High School. I was interested in becoming an auto repair technician and this motivated me to enroll in college. When I was taking several automotive courses my circumstances in life changed again and this made me revaluate my goals and career. In 2006 I attended a technical college close to home to continue with my education in the Electrical Technology industry, all this while working full time in an electrical company.

For more than 8 years I worked as a journeymen electrician in the electrical construction industry, some of the projects included in my background are schools, hospitals, data centers, apartment complex, residential homes and as an electrical service technician.In 2015 I achieved my master electrical license, and later in 2019 I obtained my electrical contractor license and became to own Jesdel Electric LLC.

 Alberto Delacruz Owner
